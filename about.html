A Province Like No Other

Welcome to Tara Pangasinan, your official digital gateway to the breathtaking wonders of the Pangasinan province. From the world-renowned Hundred Islands National Park to the pristine golden sands of Patar Beach, our mission is to elevate local tourism while preserving our rich cultural heritage.

Founded by passionate locals, we believe that every journey should be seamless and unforgettable. Whether you are an adventure seeker, a history buff, or someone looking to unwind by the sea, we provide you with the tools to curate your perfect itinerary.
